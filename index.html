Excellence + Innovation

I plan effectively and follow through diligently. I don't get distracted easily. I don't allow fluff into my brain nor circle of influence. I say "No" to any project or opportunity that doesn't lead me steadily closer to my desired outcomes. I am patient, thoughtful, wise, and planned in my actions, knowing that greater things are on the horizon.
